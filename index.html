Our approach

Built different, on purpose.

Unwrap is ad-free and independent. We don't take money from food companies, we don't sell your data, and we don't use scare tactics to keep you hooked.

We use a sliding-scale subscription — every tier unlocks the same features. You pick what works for you. That's it.

The goal is simple: better information, not better marketing. We show what's in your food and let you decide what to do with that.
